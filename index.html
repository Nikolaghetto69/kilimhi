hello, is Meow

Я -

Meow is a programmer who started coding through early fascination with video game logic. After graduating with degrees in software engineering and AI, the career began at a major tech company working on search algorithms. Subsequent roles included developing recommendation systems and leading mobile development teams. The programmer later founded an educational startup focused on teaching children coding through interactive games. Notable achievements include creating an open-source machine learning library, winning a major hackathon with a mental health chatbot project, and being a regular speaker at tech conferences. Outside work, Meow volunteers teaching programming to underprivileged children, enjoys photography (especially of cats, which inspired the pseudonym), and maintains an active lifestyle. The personal philosophy centers on using code as a tool for positive change in the world.
